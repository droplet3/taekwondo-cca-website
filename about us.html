Our mission

At NYP Taekwondo, our CCA aims to foster a strong sense of camaderie and strengthen our passion in honing our martial arts through developing our skills and building bonds. As a taekwondo practicioner, discipline and respect is of our core values and should be our utmost priority

Our history

The NYP Taekwondo club was founded in 1993, a year after the founding of Nan Yang Poly,

Our CCA has won several awards including the most recent gold medal at the pesta sukan championships 2025

The CCA originaly started out with only 29 members, however over the years, the member count has grown to over 200 members and 100 alumnis

Our values

Courtesy (Ye Ui):
This tenet emphasizes politeness, respect for others, and good manners. It encourages students to treat others with the same respect they expect to receive.

Integrity (Yom Chi):
Integrity means being honest with oneself and others, behaving ethically, and maintaining a strong moral compass. It's about being true to your word and your actions.

Perseverance (In Nae):
Perseverance is the ability to persist and not give up, even when faced with challenges or difficulties. It's about pushing through obstacles and developing the strength to overcome setbacks.

Self-Control (Guk Gi):
Self-control involves managing one's emotions, impulses, and actions. It's about maintaining composure and making thoughtful decisions, even under pressure.

Indomitable Spirit (Baekjul Boolgul):
This tenet refers to the courage and determination to stand up for what is right, to persevere in the face of adversity, and to never surrender. It's about having the inner strength to overcome challenges and achieve one's goals.
