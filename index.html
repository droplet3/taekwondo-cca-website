Taekwondo

NYP Taekwondo brings members together who are passionate about learning and practising this form of Martial Arts. A combat sport which means “the way of kicking and punching”, Taekwondo is characterised by its emphasis on head-height kicks, jumping and spinning kicks and fast kicking techniques. Kyorugi (sparring) and Poomsae (pattern) will be taught under the guidance of our coach. NYP Taekwondo participates in the annual POL-ITE Games.
